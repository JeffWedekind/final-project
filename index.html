Rivalry Jiu Jitsu's Resident DJ

Jeff 'El Jeffe' Wedekind

He has always had a passion for music. At the age of 17 years old he saw someone DJing at a rave. That next day Jeff was on a mission to learn how to DJ. Eventually he would make his way up north to Seattle DJing in the Club Circuits. In 2004 he became the Southern Oregon Guitar Center Regional Battle Champion. Jeff has shared the stage with many artists. Upon moving back to Fresno, CA. Jeff has had the opportunity to DJ on B95, Q97, and also DJ major sporting events (Grappling of the Badges). Jeff has recently joined the Brolic Army under Rob Swift of the X-ecutioners to build on his knowledge of turntablism.

ROC RAIDA OF THE X-EXECUTIONERS.

The X-Ecutioners are a group of American hip hop DJs/Turntablists from New York City, New York. The group formed in 1989 and currently consists of three DJs, including Total Eclipse, DJ Boogie Blind, DJ Precision. Original members of the group included Mista Sinista, Rob Swift, and Roc Raida (who died in September 2009).
