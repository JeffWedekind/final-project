Jeff "El Jeffe" Wedekind

I started playing the viola in 5th grade.
I've always had a passion for music.
I played the viola throughout middle school,
and up into my sophomore year of high school.
When i was 17 years old I saw someone DJing at a rave.
After that day I was on a mission to learn how to DJ.
Eventually I would make my way up north to Seattle, WA.
Throughout my early 20's I DJ'ed within the Seattle Dance Scene.
In 2004 I became the Southern Oregon
Guitar Center Regional Battle Champion.
I've shared the stage with many artists
and truly indulged in the music culture.
As I moved back to Fresno, CA. I've had the opportunity to DJ on B95, Q97,
and also DJ major sporting events. The musical journey will never stop.
It just keeps getting better and better.
I have recently joined the Brolic Army under Rob Swift of the X-ecutioners
to build on my knowledge of turntablism.
